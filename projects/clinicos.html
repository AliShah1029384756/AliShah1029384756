ClinicOS

Specialized clinic and therapy operations management system for healthcare providers.

Private (FYP) Private Case Study

Public Safety Note

This page shares case-study level architecture and impact only. Sensitive datasets, internal credentials, and implementation-level details are intentionally withheld.

Project Overview

ClinicOS is an independent clinic operations management platform designed for modern therapy and healthcare workflows. It provides therapy providers and clinic administrators with comprehensive tools to manage patient workflows, appointment scheduling, therapy session tracking, billing, and resource allocation efficiently.

Case Study Snapshot

  • Built for clinic teams that need one operational system instead of disconnected scheduling and billing tools
  • Supports structured patient, staff, and session coordination with clear workflow boundaries
  • Demonstrates healthcare-oriented product thinking with maintainable business logic

Business Problem

Therapy clinics and healthcare providers struggle with disconnected systems for scheduling, billing, patient records, and staff management. ClinicOS unifies these operations into a single, intuitive platform designed for the unique needs of multi-provider therapy clinics.

Key Features

  • Patient Management: Centralized patient records with medical history, therapy needs, and provider assignments
  • Advanced Scheduling: Multi-provider appointment scheduling with conflict detection and cancellation management
  • Session Management: Structured therapy session documentation, progress notes, and outcome tracking
  • Billing & Invoicing: Automated billing based on session types, insurance integration, payment tracking
  • Staff Management: Provider schedules, specialization tracking, availability management, and team coordination
  • Clinic Analytics: Revenue reports, patient outcomes, provider performance, and operational efficiency metrics

Technology Stack

Frontend

React.js with advanced state management for complex clinic workflows and real-time updates

Backend

Node.js with Express.js, complex business logic for scheduling optimization and billing calculations

Database

MySQL with relational schema for patient data, session history, billing records, and staff management

Integration

Payment gateway integration, calendar synchronization, insurance API connectivity

System Architecture

  • Built on AutiSmart's secure authentication layer for consistent provider identity
  • Modular billing engine supporting multiple pricing models and insurance configurations
  • Scheduler with intelligent conflict detection and patient-provider matching algorithms
  • Real-time notification system for appointment changes and urgent updates
  • Comprehensive audit trail for compliance with healthcare regulations

Learning Outcomes

  • Complex business logic implementation for healthcare operations
  • Building systems that handle concurrent booking and resource constraints
  • Integrating external payment and insurance systems into web applications
  • Designing scalable database schemas for multi-entity clinic environments
  • Real-time features: notifications, live updates, concurrent session handling
